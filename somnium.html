Somnium

product design


The concept of this project was to create a shower gel to be given as a gift with the Vogue Portugal Magazine.

The concept was the surrealism, so the final result resembles a bottle that is melting, creating a textured surface that gives the user a sensory experience.

Somnium is based on surrealism and the dream world, so I got inspired by the famous surrealist painter Salvador Dali, deciding to incorporate the melting clock, which is a common and well-known element of his paintings, on the shower gel, resulting on a bottle that resembles melting - hence the different texture on the lower part of the bottle.

Gradients are used for the label, because these are really similar to auras and are a representation of relaxation without having a clear pattern, just like when you're dreaming, you can't tell exactly what you're seeing.

The last photo is a mockup of the box that will contain the shower gel and the Vogue magazine.
