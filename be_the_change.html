Be the Change

an educational board game

For this specific project, we were asked to create a board game from cratch, we would have to come up with a concept and build everything from there, from the rules to the actual game and assets.

The sustainable development of our planet is an extremely important topic that should be discussed by everyone including children, in the sense that being aware of our planet's problems since a young age could make a positive difference regarding children's behaviour and knowledge about the earth.

With that in mind, I decided to create a boardgame that could, at the same time, entertain people of all ages and educate them (specifically children) around the world about the sustainable development goals.

After a lot of research and sketches, the concept of this game was successfully created and it consists in collecting resources and with those resources, fulfill the sustainable development goals. With this game, more people will be aware of these problems and goals, while, at the same time, having fun and bonding with family and friends.
