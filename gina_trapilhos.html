Gina e Trapilhos

branding + product design

Gina e Trapilhos is a brand that sells products related to arts & crafts, from baskets to rag dolls (the most common and well-known product).

The aim of this project was to do a rebranding for brands that were part of the Agakan Foundation, and later create a "portable wrokstation" for the artisian (creator of the brand) to work on a daily baisis.

Since the artisian of this brand does not have a physical store where she sells her work, her way of doing business consits on taking her work to places that want to recieve her, like fairs or exebhitions, so she can showcase her work and try to sell it.

Therefore, the final product consists in a showcase where she could store her materials - buttons, sewing threads and all the other materials she uses in a daily basis. It can also storage some of her dolls as a displayed example, in case this working station is also used to demonstrate to the customer their material and style options, plus the final product. Moreover, it also includes a removable trolley for easier transportation. - check the renders and photos of the final "workstation".
